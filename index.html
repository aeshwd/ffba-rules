Rules for Players

  • Timing and Attendance: Players must strictly adhere to their training schedule.
  • Dress Code: Proper sports attire is mandatory, including a T-shirt, shorts, and non-marking shoes.
  • Players not in the proper attire will not be allowed to train.
  • Equipment: Players must bring their own rackets and water bottles.
  • Discipline: Players must maintain discipline and follow the instructions of coaches at all times.
  • Arguments, foul language, or any form of disrespect will not be tolerated.
  • Health and Safety: Players should inform the coach immediately in case of any injury or discomfort.
  • Training with an injury or illness is not allowed unless approved by the coach.
  • Court Usage: No sitting, running, or loitering around the court boundaries.
  • Ensure courts are kept clean and tidy; dispose of waste in dustbins.
  • Responsibility for Pick-Up and Drop-Off: After and before training, the academy or any of its members are not responsible for any misfortune. Parents or guardians are fully responsible for picking up and dropping off players at the scheduled times.
